What is the HBO 80s Database?

The HBO 80s Database is a searchable web page with a complete record of everything that ever aired on HBO from Jan 1, 1980 to Dec 31, 1989.

You'll be able to pick any date in the 1980s and see what HBO aired that day. Search up a movie or program to see exactly when it aired, and how many times that decade.
