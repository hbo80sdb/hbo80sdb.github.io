The last minute re-broadcast of the Superfight between Marvin Hagler vs. Sugar Ray Leonard messed with the schedule

The 3:30 pm re-broadcast of the Superfight between Hagler and Leonard was a last minute change that replaced a scheduled airing of the film Head Office. The schedule for the rest of the night was also changed, according the April 18th, 1987 listing in The New York Times.
