The Superfight: Marvin Hagler vs. Sugar Ray Leonard

An expensive last minute addition to the schedule.

HBO had produced the special Road to the Superfight: Hagler vs. Leonard to air before the fight, but did not have the rights to air the fight live, which was watched via Closed-Circuit Television on April 6, 1987. HBO then made a deal to re-broadcast the fight 5 times in the week following the bout.

The following are the programs that were originally scheduled in the guide, but were replaced by the fight broadcast:

  • April 11The Hollywood Knights
  • April 12Johnny Dangerously
  • April 14Angel (1983)
  • April 16Ninja III: The Domination
  • April 18Head Office
